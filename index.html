About me

Maria was born and bred in the city of Westbrook, ME. An exuberant personality she was a creator from a very young age, learning a favorite style - cartooning - from her father after he gifted her her first specialty pen set. With a magnetic personality she excelled in projects for the student body in High School, and was a rising star in sports statewide as an MVP field hockey goalie with many trophies in her case. Already showing promise in her first love of art, she was commissioned by her home city of Westbrook to create the artwork for a new community center. Returning home to Maine after graduating college Maria sought work in another area she loved - fashion - and would continue working in the fashion industry for many years to come.
In 2019, she was by all accounts already well accomplished in her field, and as if called by her ever enthusiastic personality she decided to deke back towards her first love of creating art and left her job in the fashion industry to begin anew in the city of Portland, Maine, just adjacent to her hometown of Westbrook. Since then she has been busy creating and learning new artistic disciplines, expanding greatly on her first love of cartooning! Now considering herself a multi-media artist, Maria has been fortunate to be able to share her artwork through displaying it in home studio and by request in visual spaces around the city in shops, restaurants and share spaces, as well as during hugely popular regular events called "Artwalks," hosted by the city of Portland. Here artists from all over Maine are free to showcase, sell and share their artwork with the public.
In 2020 Maria was commissioned by the city of Portland take part in the creation of a "Portland" city name mural as part of a push to revitalize the downtown arts district. Thrilled to be be granted a chance to participate, Marai brought together a group of amazing local artists and together they designed and painted a beautiful scene on the side of music venue Aura, and it has proven to be a favorite site of locals and tourists alike.
Maria looks forward to continuing on the path of her dream to make art and share it with others, and invites you to take part of the dream too. Support your local artists!
